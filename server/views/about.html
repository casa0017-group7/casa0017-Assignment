Why we create carbon intensity data visulization website?

 

The reason why our team decide to establish carbon intensity data visualization website is in "5 Steps Go" flow chart.

 
 

Step 1   Net Zero Target

In order to realize net zero target and combat climate change, all sectors of countries’ economy should take decarbonization measures to reduce their carbon emissions.



Step 2   Greenhouse Gases Protocol

The GHG(Greenhouse Gases) Protocol has defined carbon emissions of an institution/company can be divided into scope 1, scope 2 and scope 3.

Scope 2 are emissions that a company causes indirectly and come from where the energy it purchases and uses is produced.



Step 3   Expand Renewable Energy Proportion

Electricity is generated by transforming other type of energy, for instance, coal and oil are used for power generation by a process that involves burning these fossil fuels to produce heat. This heat is then used to generate electricity.

Coal mining and oil power generation are traditional methods of electricity production, but they also come with environmental issues such as air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions. Therefore, more and more places are seeking to replace coal and oil with cleaner energy to reduce adverse environmental impacts, such as using renewable energy sources such as natural gas, solar energy, and wind energy for power generation.



Step 4   Carbon Intensity

•Carbon intensity is a measure of how clean our electricity is. It refers to how many grams of carbon dioxide (CO2) are released to produce a kilowatt hour (kWh) of electricity.

•Electricity that’s generated using fossil fuels is more carbon intensive, as the process by which it’s generated creates CO2 emissions.

•Renewable energy sources, such as wind, hydro or solar power, produce next to no CO2 emissions, so their carbon intensity value is much lower and often zero.

•Using electricity with a low carbon intensity value will reduce carbon emissions overall – especially if we use it during times when the largest amounts of clean electricity are being generated.



Step 5   Data Visualization Website

Therefore, our team want to create this carbon intensity data visualization website for stakeholders to use to make wise decisions on each level.

For companies: Useful tool for corporate strategy directors to make wise decision on sustainability aspect, such as manufacture carbon-neutral products, build a zero-carbon factory and invest other corporations. Environmental-friendly products are more likely to be preferred by consumers with environmentally awareness, and brands with a sense of social responsibility that adopt environmental protection measures are more likely to win the hearts of market consumers.


For regional government: Issuing more decarbonization policies, allocate more funds to expand renewable energy’s portion to decrease carbon intensity figure which could attract more companies to take root or invest subcompany in the region, thus, more job opportunities are created for locals and talents from the whole nation. Overall, not only can it reduce local carbon emissions, but it can also attract enterprises and talents to settle down, bringing about socio-economic development.
